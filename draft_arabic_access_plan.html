Access Plan for

For life threatening emergencies dial 1-877-205-7721 to be connected to an interpreter and Cincinnati's Emergency Line (911 Dispatch) or go to the nearest emergency room.

If you need an interpreter, use the Arabic Interpreter Line for contacting Cincinnati Children's Hospital:
